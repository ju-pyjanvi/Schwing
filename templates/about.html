About Schwing

Millions of salaried Indians juggle multiple EMIs, credit cards, and personal loans — with zero clarity on what to pay first. We built Schwing to fix that.

The Problem

Financial advisors are expensive. Generic apps give generic advice. Banks have zero incentive to help you pay off debt faster. So most people just guess — paying minimum dues, watching interest pile up, never seeing the finish line.

We wanted to build something that feels like having a brilliant, honest CFO in your pocket — one that tells you exactly: pay ₹3,200 extra on your HDFC card this month, here's why.

What Schwing Does

Upload your salary slip. Enter your debts. In under 8 seconds, Schwing's AI generates a personalized month-by-month roadmap to becoming debt-free — comparing the Avalanche and Snowball methods and recommending the best one for your situation.
